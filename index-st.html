Plastic waste

The remains of human daily activities in solid or semi-solid form in the form of non-biodegradable inorganic substances which are considered useless and disposed of into the environment.

Organic waste

The waste that comes from the remains of living things that easily decomposes naturally without human intervention to decompose.

chemical waste

The remains of human daily activities in solid form in the form of non-biodegradable inorganic substances which are considered useless and disposed of into the environment..
